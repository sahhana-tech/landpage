About the event

One Fest.
Every Kind of Talent.

Euphoria is the flagship annual fest hosted by Voice of Easwari, SRM Easwari Engineering College's official event-hosting club. A single day at the OAT Auditorium packed with competitions, showcases and open floors — built for anyone who wants to compete, perform, build, or just show up and be part of it.

  • Competitions across culture, tech, and general knowledge
  • Open floor and talent showcase slots for all departments
  • Workshops and guest sessions through the day
  • Certificates and prizes for category winners
